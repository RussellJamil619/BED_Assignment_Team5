How grades work

Hygiene grades, explained

Every stall is inspected by an NEA officer and scored out of 100. The score becomes a public letter grade, valid for a year.

A

85 and above

Excellent hygiene standards.

B

70 – 84

Good, minor issues noted.

C

50 – 69

Satisfactory, needs attention.

D

Below 50

Immediate follow-up required.
